What is Road2Hire Tech?

Process

Road2Hire is a program that trains, young adults leaders into Software Engineering or Sales positions at RedVentures.

Once on the Road2Hire program, the candidates go through a rigorous training bootcamp.

Everyday

In the Tech Academy, we are challenged daily, given problems, work and lectures.

The Environment in the Tech Academy is super dynamic, and challenging; but there's a vast variety of resources within Red Ventures.

Mentors, Other Software Engineers, prior graduates, and Mark Kroh(The professor) are just a few of the resources.

What we do

The first five-weeks of the program are for personal development, we are pushed out of your comfort-zone.

Wether you are a introvert or an extrovert, you are pushed to try harder, think smart, and just try new things for your self!

We Work, We gain Experiences, We team build, We're Road2hire!
